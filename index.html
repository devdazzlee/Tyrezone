Faq's

Are the rims and tires sold together?

You can choose to buy them together or separately. Our assortment of products has both options for our customers. They can choose to buy their tires and rims separately or buy the whole wheel as a single product. The changeover will be as smooth and stylish as the rest of your vehicle.

Where can I find out what size tires my car requires?

We have professionals ready to help you. Contact us before you make a purchase on your website, and we will guide you according to your situation. Tell us about your car and driving habits so we can propose a tire and wheel combination that optimizes performance while still looking good.

Can I get tires without rims?

Sure! If you like your present wheels but still want to replace your tires for a safer, quicker, smoother, and more durable option, then our collection is the best to choose from. Have a look at our catalog or contact our support staff, and they will help.

Is it safe to use these tires all year round?

Several of our tires are all-season types, so you may drive confidently throughout the year regardless of the weather. However, ensure that you are choosing the right tires for your vehicle. If you need our help in choosing the right wheels, tires, or rims for your ride, then be sure to call us.
